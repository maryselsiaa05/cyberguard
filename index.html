About CyberGuard EDU

CyberGuard EDU is my passion project to make cybersecurity simple and practical for students. I wanted a place where anyone can learn to protect themselves online with real skills—not just theory. Through virtual labs, workshops, and easy-to-understand content, this platform helps you defend, detect, and dominate cyber threats confidently. It’s made for everyone who uses the internet, because knowing cybersecurity means being safe and strong in today’s digital world.

-MARY SELSIAA
